Privacy Policy — WA Scheduler

Last updated: March 2026

What data we collect

WA Scheduler collects only the minimum data necessary to operate:

  • Email address and name for account creation
  • WhatsApp contact names and phone numbers, imported with your explicit consent
  • Scheduled message content and delivery times you create

How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to send scheduled WhatsApp messages on your behalf. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or any purpose other than providing the service.

Chrome Extension

The WA Scheduler Chrome extension:

  • Only runs on web.whatsapp.com
  • Reads the name of the currently open chat to pre-fill the recipient field
  • Stores your authentication token locally using chrome.storage.local — this never leaves your device except to authenticate with our backend
  • Does not read, store or transmit your WhatsApp messages

Data storage

Data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted in the United States. Authentication tokens expire after 30 days.

Data deletion

You can delete your contacts and scheduled messages at any time from within the app. To request full account deletion, contact us at the email below.
